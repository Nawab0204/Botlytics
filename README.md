Botlytics - Conversational Analytics
Live Demo & Docs: https://nawab0204.github.io/Botlytics/

Botlytics is a powerful, open-source analytics platform designed to give you deep insights into your chatbot's conversations. Move beyond simple message counts and truly understand how users interact with your bot, what they're looking for, and where your bot can be improved to create a more effective and engaging user experience.

Why Botlytics?
Building a great chatbot is more than just coding responses; it's about creating a seamless conversational flow. Without the right data, you're flying blind. Botlytics provides the data-driven insights you need to:

Understand User Behavior: See exactly where users get stuck or drop off in a conversation.

Optimize Performance: Identify your most and least effective intents and responses.

Increase Engagement: Discover what your users are actually asking for and tailor your bot's capabilities to meet their needs.

Make Informed Decisions: Use concrete data, not guesswork, to guide your bot's development.

✨ Key Features
📈 Comprehensive Dashboard: Visualize all your key metrics, from message volume to user retention, in one clean and intuitive interface.

🔍 Full Conversation Tracking: Log and review every user message, intent classification, and bot response to get a complete picture of every interaction.

📊 User Engagement Metrics: Analyze session length, interaction frequency, and user flows to understand how engaging your bot truly is.

🎯 Bot Performance Analysis: Pinpoint your bot's most effective conversation paths and identify responses that lead to confusion or dead ends.

🔌 Seamless Integration: Get started in minutes with simple SDKs designed for popular chatbot platforms and frameworks.

🛠️ Supported Platforms

🚀 Getting Started
Integration is straightforward. Simply choose the SDK for your platform and follow the setup instructions to start sending conversational data to your Botlytics dashboard.

Sign Up on the Botlytics platform.

Create a New Project to get your unique API key.

Integrate the SDK into your chatbot's code.

Visualize Your Insights and start building a better bot!
